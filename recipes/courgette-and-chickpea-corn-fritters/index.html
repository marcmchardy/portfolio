Courgette & Chickpea Corn Fritters

Hearty but not too heavy, the grated courgette gives these corn fritters a satisfying bite. Enjoy on their own, with poached eggs, or with a salsa and sour cream.

Servings: 12
Time: 35 minutes
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 can of whole corn kernals in springwater
  • 1 can of drained and rinsed checkpeas
  • 1 grated courgette
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground pepper
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1/2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground fennel seed
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin seed
  1. Preheat oven to 160˚C to keep the fritters in after frying, this will also ensure they are cooked through.
  2. Mix all dried ingredients.
  3. Add all other ingredients including the springwater from the can of corn kernals.
  4. Mix thoroughly with a spoon and leave mixture to the side. Heat a pan on medium with a shallow covering of oil. I use a mix of grape seed oil and olive oil.
  5. Fry in batches of four to five, each about the size of a pikelette.
  6. Flip the fritter when it has started to turn a golden brown on the bottom side.
  7. Add the cooked fritters to a cooling rack and place in the oven for 10–15 minutes to finish cooking through.
  8. Serve with poached eggs on a bed of spinach and rocket, dressed in lemon juice and olive oil.
