To help students and teachers use Banqer High, Banqer's Highschool product to it's fullest, and reduce the time the spent handling support queries a new support and FAQ system was designed and developed. Successfully reducing the number of recieved support queries by 92%.

This solution provides students and teachers with instant answers while still allowing them to reach out if they can't find what they are looking for.

Focus on belief in recieving feedback and suggestions.
